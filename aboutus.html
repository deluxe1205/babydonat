Our History

Founded in 2023 by Cristy Alba, Baby Donat is more than just a home-based store at 114 Emma Street in Pasay City. It’s a place where simple pleasures meet extraordinary flavors. It all began with a love for homemade mini donuts, shared with family and friends. As time went on, we expanded our menu to include comforting Filipino dishes. But our passion doesn’t stop there. We've also embraced our artistic side, crafting unique handmade goods like satin flowers and money garlands to add a touch of beauty to any occasion.

Our Goal

Baby Donat is a local business dedicated to delighting customers with a variety of delicious Filipino dishes and exquisite handmade crafts. We strive to create memorable experiences by offering high-quality products and exceptional service.
